Faculty

He Sun

He Sun, Assistant Professor of Biomedical Engineering & Biophysics at Peking University in Beijing, China. His research primarily focuses on AI for scientific imaging, which tightly integrates optics, control, signal processing and machine learning for scientific discoveries.

Prior to starting at Peking University, He was a postdoctoral researcher and Amazon AI4Science Fellow at Caltech, where he mainly worked with Katie Bouman on black hole imaging. He received his Ph.D. in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from Princeton University in 2019 (advised by N. Jeremy Kasdin) and his bachelor’s degree in Engineering Mechanics and Economics from Peking University in 2014.
