Student projects

We are looking for self-motivated students for the following projects. We have a history of mentoring undergraduate and graduate students interested in research, with many of our alumni becoming PhD/Master students in top programs, including UCLA, WashU, ETH. If you are interested in a given project, please apply by emailing your resume and a short description of your interests to the project supervisor

Opening: We are open to self-motivated Postdoc / PhD / Master / research interns candidates and other potential collaboration.
Please do not hesitate to contact us by emailing hesun@pku.edu.cn!

Diffusion Posterior Sampling with Refined Prompts

Status: available

Description: Modern text-to-image generative models provide powerful data-driven priors for computational imaging and inverse problems. We have a board interest in this topic, and we are also dedicated to leveraging these generative priors for solving various scientific imaging problems.

Supervision: Weimin Bai (weiminbai@stu.pku.edu.cn), He Sun.
Areas: Diffusion Model, Computational Imaging, Text-to-Image Generation

3D Representations

Status: available

Description: We focus on cutting-edge 3D representations and 3D generations. There are many interesting scientific applications that are eager for fast, efficient and accurate 3D modeling techniques.
